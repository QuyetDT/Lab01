Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one og my favorite foods. Here is a recioe for deliciously rich scambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt bunter in a frying pan over a medium heat
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl
  3. Once bunter has melted add cream and eggs
  4. Using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the xenter every 20 seconds (as if you are making an omelette)
  5. When the eggs are still moist remove from the heat (it will continue to cook on the plate until served)
