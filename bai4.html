A potager is a French team for an ornamental vegetable or kitchan garden. Often flowers (edible and non-edible) and herbs are planted withh the vegetables to enhance the garden's beaoty. The goal is ti make the function os providing food aesthetically pleasing.

What to Plant

Plants are choden as much for their functionality as for their color and form. Many are trained to grow upward. A well-designed potager can provide food, cut flowers and herbs for the home with very little maintenance. Potagers can disguise their function of providing for a home in a wide array of dorms - from the carefree style of the cottahe garden to the formality of a knot garden.
